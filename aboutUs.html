Welcome to
SVBH School

Back in 2004, when there were hardly any educational institutions in and around thumkunta; when there were difficulties to commute to schools they provide quality education; when education was a privilege to few people in the locality; our visionary founders B.JANGA REDDY and G HANUMANTHA REDDY, identified the gap in the locality, established SRI VIGNANABHARATHI High School to serve children around the institution with finest quality education with innovative and child centric learning. We are proud to announce that SRI VIGNANABHARATHI HIGH SCHOOL was first school in Thumkunta. to setup Computer lab, Science lab and Mathematics lab inside the campus. We are also the first in the locality to introduce digital classrooms to all the classes. With over 18 years of experience in creating stars that create an impact in the society, we will continue creating stars that will be a part of India’s Best progress.

Our Mission

SRI VIGNANA BHARATHI High School strives everyday:

  • - To provide finest quality education, discipline and boost self-confidence in a child to help them realize their dreams.

  • - To createchild centric effective learning programs (Activitybased learning and customized revision classes) by establishing behavioral support and the social culture needed to achieve social, emotional and academic success.

  • - To inspire in children the love of learning, thereby empowering them to innovate and become life-long learners.

Our Vision

To nurture our students as useful citizens and future leaders for inculcating in them the culture of excellence, innovation, creativity, high moral values, passion for life-long learning and a global perspective.
